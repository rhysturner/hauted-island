🏝️ Haunted Island

You are stranded on a haunted island.
Some skeletons are friendly and some are hostile — but you can't tell by looking!

Collect holy water ⚗️, torches 🔦, and silver daggers 🗡️ scattered across the island. Press F to use your held item on a nearby skeleton and destroy it.

Press T to talk to a nearby skeleton. Type your reply — if they like what you say they turn friendly and may heal you; anger them and they will hunt you down.

Defeat or befriend all hostile skeletons to escape the island!

WASD / Arrow Keys to move  |  F to use item  |  T to talk
