🏝️ Haunted Island

You are stranded on a haunted island.
Skeletons roam the island and will chase you on sight!

Collect holy water ⚗️, torches 🔦, and silver daggers 🗡️ scattered across the island. Press F to use your held item on a nearby skeleton and destroy it.

Destroy all skeletons to escape the island!

WASD / Arrow Keys to move  |  F to use item
